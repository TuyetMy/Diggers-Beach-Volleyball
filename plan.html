Mission

The Diggers Volleyball website is intended to get more younger players to play volleyball, thus increasing revenue to the family run business. For Interested parties it provides information about the sport and the opportunity to sign up with a team and for existing members it serves as a blackboard with announcements and current competitions.

Success Evaluation

After 3 months sign-ups will be counted to evaluate the number of new members.

Target Audience

High school children and young adults between 12 and 35 years old, local residents. The minimalistic website design and colours will attract youngsters (e.g. Instagram, twitter)
